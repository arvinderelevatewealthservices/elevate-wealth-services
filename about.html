Who We Are

Your Financial Growth
Is Our Purpose

Elevate Wealth Services is a premium financial advisory firm based in Amritsar, Punjab. We are AMFI registered, IRDAI certified, and SEBI compliant — a fully regulated platform dedicated to helping individuals and families build, grow, and protect their wealth.

Founded on the belief that expert financial guidance should be accessible, transparent, and genuinely client-first, we have served hundreds of investors across India with personalized strategies for mutual funds, insurance, and trading.

Our approach combines regulatory rigor with human understanding — we take the time to know your goals, understand your risk appetite, and craft financial solutions that grow with you at every stage of life.
